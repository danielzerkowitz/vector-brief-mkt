How It Works

Automated speed. Human judgment.

  1. Share contact details. Tell us who should be contacted, the likely destination, and the broad requirement.
  2. Scope the requirement. A specialist confirms the traveler profile, route, dates, sensitivity, and briefing depth before any package is prepared.
  3. Prepare and verify. Vector Brief compiles the intelligence draft, then a security and local intelligence specialist reviews and validates the briefing before release.
